Academic Curriculum

Our Courses

Tabla Courses

Beginner Tabla

Learn the History, Importance, Fundamentals of Tabla playing, Basics of Rhythm, Fingering techniques to create different sounds, foundational Bols (syllables) to build a strong base.

  • Duration: 4-5 months
  • Focus: Finger technique, Recitation, Basic Rhythms
  • For: Beginners with little/no experience
Enroll Now

Intermediate Level-1

Build on foundational skills & become a successful accompanist. Start conversing with foot-tapping beats enriched with melody and speed using Rhythmic Patterns.

  • Duration: 1.5 – 2.0 years
  • Focus: Basic Taals, Variations, Multi-genre Accompaniment
  • For: Graduates of Beginner course
Enroll Now

Intermediate Level-2

Shape up into a performer. Extensive training in Gharana-based classical compositions to achieve fluidity in Tabla Vocabulary and Practice.

  • Duration: 2 – 4 years
  • Focus: Advanced Taals, Gharana Repertoire
  • For: Graduates of Intermediate-1
Enroll Now

Advanced Tabla

Master the art of Tabla! For passionate learners ready for a lifelong association. Engage in advanced techniques, Concert accompaniment, and Solo performance.

  • Duration: 5+ years
  • Focus: Advanced Techniques, Solo Performance
  • For: Graduates of Intermediate-2
Enroll Now

Vocal Courses

Beginner Vocal

Start your music journey with foundational training in basic swaras and alankars to build a strong, melodic base.

  • Duration: 3-4 months
  • Focus: Voice modulation, Swaras, Alankaars
  • For: Beginners with little/no experience
Enroll Now

Intermediate Vocal

Deepen your skills with additional Ragas by exploring expressive singing techniques for classical and semi-classical singing styles.

  • Duration: 1 – 1.5 years
  • Focus: More Ragas, Bandishes, Alap
  • For: Graduates of Beginner course
Enroll Now

Advanced Vocal

Prepare for professional performances with advanced vocal training and Mastery of complex Ragas.

  • Duration: 5+ years
  • Focus: Mastery in creativity, Gharana Repertoire
  • For: Graduates of Intermediate course
Enroll Now
