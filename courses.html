Our Courses

Tabla Courses

Beginner Tabla

Learn the History, Importance, Fundamentals of Tabla playing, Basics of Rhythm, Fingering techniques to create different sounds, foundational Bols (syllables) to build a strong base on Vocabulary of Tabla in Music.

  • Duration: Typically 4-5 months
  • Focus: Finger technique, Recitation, Basic Rhythms, Vocabulary, simple compositions
  • Suitable for: Beginners with Little or No experience in music
Enroll Now

Intermediate Level-1 Tabla

Build on your foundational skills & Apply them to graduate & become a successful accompanist. Start becoming conversant with foot tapping beats enriched with melody and speed, by using the Rhythmic Patterns and Variations creatively. (Stage performance opportunity will be given)

  • Duration: Typically 1.5 – 2.0 years
  • Focus: Basic Taals, Variations, Multi-genre Song accompaniment skills (Bhajans, Bollywood, Ghazal, Tabla Covers, etc)
  • Suitable for: Learners who have completed the Beginners course
Enroll Now

Intermediate Level-2 Tabla

Start shaping up into a performer by embarking on a journey of advanced Taals. Extensive training in Gharana based classical compositions till you master them to achieve fluidity in Tabla Vocabulary and Practice. (Stage performance opportunity will be given)

  • Duration: Typically 2 – 4 years
  • Focus: Advanced Taals, Fixed long & Short compositions, Mastery of Tabla Gharana based repertoire, Basic Classical accompaniment
  • Suitable for: Learners who have completed the Intermediate-1 course
Enroll Now

Advanced Tabla

Master the art of Tabla..! Only for learners who are passionate about Tabla. Be ready for a lifelong association with Music where you engage in learning advanced techniques, Concert level accompaniment skills, Independent Tabla Solo performance skills and more…

  • Duration: 5+ years to endless learning..!
  • Focus: Advanced Techniques, Advanced accompaniment skills, Mastery in creative / on-the-fly compositions, concert performance level preparation
  • Suitable for: Learners who have completed the Intermediate-2 course
Enroll Now

Vocal Courses

  • Duration: Typically 3-4 months
  • Focus: Voice modulation, Swaras, Alankaars, Basic Ragas
  • Suitable for: Beginners with Little or No experience in music

Intermediate Vocal

Deepen your skills with additional Ragas by exploring expressive singing techniques for classical and semi-classical singing styles. (Stage performance opportunity will be given)

  • Duration: Typically 1 – 1.5 years
  • Focus: More Ragas, Bandishes, introduction to Alap
  • Suitable for: Learners who have completed the Beginners course
